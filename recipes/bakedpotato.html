The Perfect Baked Potato

The search for the easy and delicious baked potato is over!

This potato is creamy, salty, crunchy, and delicious! It's also very easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium/large russet potato (or any baking potato of your choice)
  • 3 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tbsp vegetable or olive oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400° F
  2. Rinse and scrub potato
  3. In a bowl, coat the potato with oil and then rub on the sea salt
  4. Place potato on baking sheet and place in oven for 1 hour
  5. Remove from of and slice the potato lengthwise down the middle
  6. Add desired ingredients to potato, e.g. butter, sour cream, shredded cheese, and/or bacon
  7. Enjoy!
