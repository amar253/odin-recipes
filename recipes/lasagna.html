Lasagna

This lasagna will knock your socks off!

It's everything you could ever want in a pasta

  • Amazing italian pasta
  • Delicious italian-seasoned meat
  • A robust tomato sauce
  • And so much CHEESY GOODNESS!!!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of your preferred tomato sauce
  • 1 500g package
  • 1/2 pound 80/20 ground beef
  • 1/2 pound italian sausage
  • 1 pound italian blend cheese
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese

Steps:

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef and sausage
  3. Add the sauce to your beef
  4. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  5. Preheat oven & Assemble the lasagna
  6. Bake
  7. Cool and serve!
